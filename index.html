About the Event

Join us at the sankranti celebrations 2025 for a day filled with laughter, nostalgia, and exciting activities.Sankranti is the only Hindu festival based on the solar calendar instead of the lunar. Makar Sankranti is the celebration of the sun's journey from the Southern to the Northern Hemisphere and is considered an auspicious time. Makar translates as 'Capricorn' and Sankranti means 'transition'.
